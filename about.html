About Farmers Connect Lesotho

Our mission is to empower local farmers and provide customers with fresh, high-quality produce directly from the source.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Farmers Connect Lesotho began as a small initiative to help local farmers in Maseru District sell their produce directly to consumers, bypassing middlemen who often took most of the profits.

What started as a WhatsApp group connecting 15 farmers to 50 customers has now grown into Lesotho's premier digital farming marketplace, serving thousands of farmers and customers across all 10 districts.

Our platform is built by Basotho, for Basotho, with the goal of strengthening local agriculture and food security while ensuring fair compensation for our hardworking farmers.

Our Mission

To create a sustainable digital ecosystem that connects Lesotho's farmers directly with consumers, eliminating unnecessary intermediaries, reducing food waste, and ensuring fair prices for both producers and buyers.

Our Vision

To become the leading agricultural marketplace in Lesotho, transforming the way food is produced, distributed, and consumed while supporting the growth of sustainable farming practices and food security nationwide.

Our Core Values

Community First

We prioritize the needs of both farmers and customers, building relationships that benefit all members of our community.

Integrity

We operate with honesty and transparency in all our dealings, ensuring trust between all parties on our platform.

Sustainability

We promote farming practices that protect the environment while ensuring long-term viability for agricultural businesses.

Quality

We maintain high standards for all products on our platform, ensuring customers receive fresh, healthy, and authentic farm produce.

Local Empowerment

We're committed to strengthening Lesotho's agricultural sector and keeping wealth within our communities.

Innovation

We continuously improve our platform to better serve our users, leveraging technology to solve real agricultural challenges.
